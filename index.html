While some companies hire consultants for weeks, most impactful insights can be delivered in just 30 minutes. It's the 80-20 rule (aka Pareto Principle). My audits are focused, actionable, and designed to give you maximum value without the extra fluff.

How much does it cost to add more ads or landing pages?

The first ad/landing page combo costs $495, and additional ones are $350 each. No contracts or ongoing fees. For most SaaS companies, even one new customer can cover the cost of the audit.

Can you audit a landing page or ad that isn’t live yet?

Yes! Pre-launch is a great time to audit your landing page designs or concepts. Simply book your audit and provide the details in the intake form.

Absolutely. Your audit will be sent via a private link for you to view and download. It won’t be shared anywhere else.
